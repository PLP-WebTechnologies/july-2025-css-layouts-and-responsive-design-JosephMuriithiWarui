About Mountains

Mountains are some of the most breathtaking natural features on Earth. They are formed by geological processes such as tectonic plate movements and volcanic activity. From snow-capped peaks to green valleys, mountains provide habitats for diverse wildlife and are home to many cultures around the world.

Why Mountains Matter

  • They influence weather and climate patterns.
  • Provide fresh water sources through rivers and glaciers.
  • Offer adventure and tourism opportunities.
  • Support unique ecosystems and biodiversity.
